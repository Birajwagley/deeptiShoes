Company Profile

Mother Company Profile

The Cedar was registered in 1986 in China. After many years of accumulation of experience, cedar gradually formed its own unique design style. On the basis of perfect classic products, Cedar was integrated with the original creation thinking and modern principles of material choice of shoes, ensuring the product quality standard. Cedar with its professional functional shoe design, is a friend for trekkers seeking a blend of comfort, durability, and performance. Renowned for its exceptional features, Cedar has become widely known brand of outdoor sports.

Today, cedar goods offers hiking shoes, work shoes, casual shoes and hiking shoes with four series more than 100 varieties, in addition with more sandals, ski-wear, backpacks and other products. Cedar is always in line with comfort, durability and rugged design object, to the perfect combination between science and technology, launched to let all people to experience this innovation serving dazzling design , especially in the series of hiking, to all people who love outdoor sports as perfect choice! Today, the distribution network has spread all over the country and Taiwan areas, mainly exported to Europe and the United States.

Nepal Company Profile

Cedar Trekking Shoes, a distinguished name in high-performance trekking footwear, has officially entered the Nepalese market. On 1st January, 2024, Cedar Trekking Shoes inaugurated its franchise operations in Nepal, marking a major milestone in the country’s trekking and outdoor gear industry, at its newly established store located at the heart of Kathmandu’s commercial hub, Nepa Complex, Newroad. D Cube Traders has been appointed as the sole authorized distributor for Cedar Trekking Shoes in Nepal. This strategic partnership ensures that local adventurers have access to the brand’s premium trekking shoes and gear, which are renowned for their durability, comfort, and performance.

Years Experience

Cedar Trekking Shoes offers a diverse and specialized range of footwear designed to meet the rigorous demands of trekking enthusiasts. From rugged hiking boots engineered for challenging terrains to versatile trail shoes that offer both comfort and protection, Cedar provides a comprehensive selection tailored to support various outdoor activities. By bringing its high-quality products to Nepal, Cedar Trekking Shoes aims to empower adventure seekers with the gear they need to tackle the country's diverse and demanding landscapes. This move signifies a new chapter for adventurers, offering them the confidence and readiness needed for their next journey into the great outdoors.
